This demo aims to illustrate how to bind some common control templates to sources in the MVVM way in WPF. Control templates mainly include TreeView, DataGrid, ComboBox, ListView, and more. This demo is simple, without complex interaction logic, as shown in the following pictures. 

![Image text](https://github.com/Larissa1990/WPFdemo/blob/master/pics/pages.gif)

As we all know, WPF is powerful and flexible, and I'm a beginner. Therefore, it may not be the best way to realize these pages. This demo may be updated constantly.
